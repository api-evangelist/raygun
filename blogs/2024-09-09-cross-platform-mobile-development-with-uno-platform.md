Xamarin has been a popular choice for cross-platform mobile development, but Microsoft’s shift to .NET MAUI has developers seeking alternatives. Uno Platform is an open-source .NET framework for building apps across mobile, desktop, web, and embedded devices. Raygun CEO John-Daniel Trask interviewed Uno Platform’s Francois Tanguay and Sasha Krsmanovic on a recent Founder & Friends podcast .
